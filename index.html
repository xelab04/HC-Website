Hack Club has as goal to revolutionise making. There has never been a better time to be a maker, a creator. All you need is a laptop and an internet connection. While clubs exist all around the world catering to music or science or arts, the stereotypical programmer is seen as someone is isolated in the dark with cryptic symbols flashing on their screen. Hack Club aims to empower makers to do what they do best in a large community of like-minded creators.

Benefits of Hack Club

Discover What Hack Club Has To Offer!

Maker Centered

Hack Club is centered around makers and creators in the digital sphere. Dedicated to technological creativity.

Hands-On Approach

Unlike school, where hands-on projects are rare, Hack Club gives more opportunities to get your hands dirty in code.

Exciting Activities

Hack Club aims to bring learning to the table in a new and fresh fashion, replacing boring lectures and speeches with thrilling activities.

Massive Community

Hack Club reuintes makers under a single giant community. Talk to like-minded creators from all over the globe.

Peer-Based Learning

Instead of traditional teacher-student teaching, Hack Club favours learning from your peers, promoting equality.

Free Goodies

Hack Club offers a suite of premium software for free, as well as a vast amount of goodies of all sorts.
